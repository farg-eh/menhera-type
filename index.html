Why learn how to touch type ?

touch typing means typing on the keyboard without looking at the keys,
its nice to have skill for anyone and a very important skill for
people who work in a field that requires a lot of typing.

to keep you motivated, here is a list reasons of
why should you learn to touch type :

  • Productivity :
    typing faster means you will finish you tasks much faster,
    which might give you extra time to do other things, or help you produce more content in the same amount of time.

  • Synchronization :
    when your typing speed matches your thinking speed
    this will minimize the possibility of you getting distracted or loosing you chain of thoughts while slowly typing your ideas

  • Fun :
    typing on the keyboard is like a sport it becomes very satisfying when you
    learn how to do it properly, this will help you become more passionate and,
    motivated about your job or hobby and this passion will definitely make you a better writer, programmer, etc..
